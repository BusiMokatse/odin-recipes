Description

Malva pudding is a traditional south african spongy dessert served with either custard, ice cream or both.

Ingredients

    Pudding

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespons apricot jam
  • 0.75 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 0.33 cup milk

    • Sauce

  • 0.75 cup fresh cream
  • 100g butter
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celcius.
  2. Grease an 8x8 baking dish.
  3. Beat the eggs and 1 cup sugar then add the jam.
  4. Melt butter and add to the egg mixture.
  5. Add the flour, baking powder and salt to the egg mixture. Mix well.
  6. Pour the batter into the baking dish.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes until golden.
  8. In a saucepan, melt 100 g butter.
  9. Add the sugar and cream and simmer for 15 minutes.
  10. Pour over the baked pudding once it is done.
