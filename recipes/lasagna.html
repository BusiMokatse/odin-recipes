Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish mabe by layering sheets of pasta, cooked ground meat and a bechamel sauce. It is topped with loads of cheese and baked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ground beef
  • 0.5 cup diced carrot
  • 0.5 cup diced onion
  • 0.5 cup diced green pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 0.5 cup dry red wine
  • 2 cups vegetable broth, divided into 2 cups
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 0.5 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup grated parmesean
  • 500g lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. In a stockpot, add the olive oil and sautee the onion, green pepper and carro for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook for 10 minutes until the meat is browned.
  3. Add the red wine, 1 cup of vegetable broth, and tomato puree and simmer for an hour.
  4. Add the second cup of vegetable broth and simmer for another hour.
  5. Set the meat aside.
  6. In a sauce pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  7. Add the flour and whisk continuously until golden.
  8. Add 1 cup milk while stirring continuously.
  9. Add the remaining milk and simmer for 10 minutes.
  10. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celcius
  11. In a 9x13 baking dish, spread a little meat.
  12. Add a layer of pasta and top with meat and bechamel.
  13. Continue layering the pasta, meat and bechamel and top off the last layyer with the parmesean cheese.
  14. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
  15. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
