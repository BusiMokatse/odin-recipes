Garden Salad

Description

A fresh garden salad. Perfect for accompanying any meal or on its own.

Ingredients

    Salad

  • 200g croutons
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 cucumber
  • 200g baby tomatoes
  • 250g feta cheese
  • 250g olives
  • 0.5 red onion

    • Vinaigrette

  • 0.5 cup olive oil
  • 0.25 balsamic vinegar
  • black pepper to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegetables.
  2. Roughly chop the lettuce.
  3. Cut the cucumber in half lengthwise and cut it into half circles.
  4. Cut the tomatoes in half.
  5. Thinly slice the red onion.
  6. Add all the vegetables to a salad bowl along with the olives.
  7. In a seperate bowl, mix the olive oil, balsamic, salt, pepper, and lemon juice to make a vinaigrette.
  8. pour the Vinaigrette over the salad and toss the salad.
  9. Crumble the feta over the salad and add the croutons.
